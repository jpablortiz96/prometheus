# PROMETHEUS v0.4.0 Gemini Award Writeup

## Why Gemini matters in PROMETHEUS

PROMETHEUS uses Gemini where reasoning quality and structured interpretation matter:

- threat-intel extraction
- policy synthesis support
- behavioral prediction context
- decision-ready narrative
- regulator-facing summaries

## Product role

Gemini is not the only control. It is one part of a layered governance system.

PROMETHEUS uses Gemini alongside:

- Veea Lobster Trap deterministic DPI
- permission enforcement
- tribunal logic
- audit sealing

That creates a practical enterprise story: model intelligence is useful, but final governance is anchored in explainable controls and execution-path enforcement.

## Zero-Day Sentinel

Zero-Day Sentinel shows the strongest Gemini narrative:

1. a new threat report arrives
2. Gemini extracts defensive patterns
3. PROMETHEUS proposes policy updates
4. a safe synthetic scenario is created
5. the live gateway blocks the dangerous tool before execution

This demonstrates real business value without generating exploits or offensive instructions.
